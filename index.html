About Us

Dublin's Most Trusted Auto Repair Center

Family-owned and operated since 2004, Top Class Autos has built a reputation as Dublin 11's premier destination for honest, reliable car repairs.

What began as a single-bay operation has grown into a full-service automotive center, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Dublin and beyond. Our team of certified mechanics brings decades of combined experience working on all makes and models.

We believe in transparency, quality workmanship, and treating every vehicle as if it were our own. No upselling, no hidden fees—just honest advice and expert repairs at fair prices.

FAQ

Common Questions

How much do repairs typically cost?

Pricing varies by service and vehicle. We provide free, detailed quotes before starting any work. No hidden fees, no surprises.

How long do repairs take?

Simple services like oil changes take 30-45 minutes. Complex repairs may require 1-3 days depending on parts availability. We'll give you an accurate timeframe upfront.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. All our work comes with a comprehensive 12-month warranty for complete peace of mind.

Do I need to book an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome for simple services. For major repairs, we recommend booking ahead to ensure availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due when you collect your vehicle.

Do you offer collection and delivery?

Yes. We provide free collection and delivery within Dublin 11 for major services.
